Use Case Scenario 1: Withdrawing Money
--------------------------------------
1. The customer logs into their bank account.
2. The customer chooses to withdraw money from their account.
3. The system prompts the customer to select the account to withdraw from.
4. The customer selects the account and enters the amount to be withdrawn.
5. The system verifies the available balance in the selected account.
6. The customer confirms the withdrawal details and submits the request.
7. The system processes the withdrawal and updates the balance of the account.
8. The system sends a confirmation notification to the customer via email or SMS.
9. The customer receives the notification and verifies the withdrawal in their account history.

Use Case Scenario 2: Funds Transfer Between Accounts
-----------------------------------------------------
1. The customer logs into their bank account.
2. The customer navigates to the "Transfer Funds" section.
3. The system prompts the customer to select the source account and the destination account.
4. The customer selects the accounts and enters the amount to be transferred.
5. The system verifies the available balance in the source account.
6. The customer confirms the transfer details and submits the request.
7. The system processes the transfer and updates the balances of both accounts.
8. The system sends a confirmation notification to the customer via email or SMS.
9. The customer receives the notification and verifies the transfer in their account history.
